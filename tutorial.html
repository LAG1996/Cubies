Tutorial:

Here, you can name your polycube, and then give it some coordinates where the very first cube will be placed. You can put nothing in these boxes, and we'll generate a polycube for you anyway.

Add a Cube!

Insert the cube's position:

Tutorial:

Here, you can specify the cube's position relative to the first cube that was created. You can't put two cubes in the same space, so nothing will happen if you don't specify a position. Also, a new cube needs to be next to some cube that was already put down. We're working on making that a little less confusing. For now, just put a 1 in any of the boxes, and then press Submit.
